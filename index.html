ABOUT US.

Here, at Little Hill Carrot Farm, we pride oursleves on growing the best quality carrots as possible. There is an extensive process that each of our carrots groes through to be branded a "Little Hill Carrot". We examine thousands of carrot seeds to only pick the top 5% of the batch to be planted. We want each carrot to live a happy life before leaving the farm which is why we sing to our carrots everynight to ensure their happiness. This along with only watering our carrots with spring water from the Himalayas allows us the grow the carrots you love so much!

We are located in Fairview, Tennessee on 1001 carrot avenue and we offer tours every Monday morning at Dawn. Come see for yourself why there is no carrot like Little Hill Carrot's certified carrot.
